"Whispers of the Hydrangea"


In gardens where the soft winds sigh, A bloom awakens, bold and shy, With petals draped in shades so bright, A tapestry of pure delight.

Hydrangeas dance in morning light, Their colors blend, a painter's sight, From blue to pink, from white to green, In every hue, a magic scene.

They whisper secrets to the breeze, Of gentle rains and summer’s ease, In clusters, they embrace the sun, A symphony of joy begun.

With every bloom, a story told, Of fleeting time and love so bold, In the heart's garden, they will stay, A fragrant dream that will not fade away.

So let us cherish, in each glance, The beauty found in nature’s dance, For in the petals’ soft embrace, We find a moment’s timeless grace.

In the moonlight's gentle glow, A fox dances with a raccoon, Whispers of the night, their playful tune.
